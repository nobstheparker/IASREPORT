ISACA (Information Systems Audit and Control Association)

Shaping IT Governance, Security, and Risk Management

Focus Areas

  • IT Governance: Ensures IT investments align with business goals and are effectively managed.
  • Risk Management: Helps identify, assess, and mitigate IT-related risks.
  • Cybersecurity: Promotes practices to protect systems and data from cyber threats.

Key Certifications

  • CISM: Certified Information Security Manager - Focuses on managing information security programs and incident response.
  • CRISC: Certified in Risk and Information Systems Control - Specializes in IT risk management and control design.

Key Activities

  • Global Research: Publishes reports on cybersecurity and IT governance trends.
  • Conferences: Hosts events for professionals to share knowledge and discuss industry developments.
  • Training: Provides certification preparation and professional development programs.
More about ISACA
ISACA, established in 1969, is a global non-profit association focusing on IT governance, cybersecurity, and risk management. It provides certifications such as CISM, CRISC, and offers educational opportunities to help professionals excel in the fast-evolving IT field. ISACA’s research and events shape global trends and empower organizations to improve their IT strategies and security measures.
