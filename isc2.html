(ISC)² (International Information System Security Certification Consortium)

Shaping the Future of Cybersecurity Professionals

Focus Areas

  • Cybersecurity Certifications: Offers advanced certifications to validate expertise in cybersecurity.
  • Capacity Building: Provides training and resources for professionals to enhance their skills.
  • Ethical Standards: Promotes a strong Code of Ethics for cybersecurity professionals.

Key Certifications

  • CISSP: Certified Information Systems Security Professional - Focuses on broad security concepts, including risk management, security architecture, and incident response.
  • CCSP: Certified Cloud Security Professional - Specializes in securing cloud environments and cloud data.

Key Activities

  • (ISC)² CBK: Offers comprehensive resources on cybersecurity topics.
  • Ethics: Enforces a Code of Ethics for professionals.
  • Training & Events: Provides certification prep courses and hosts global events like Security Congress.
More about (ISC)²
(ISC)² is a non-profit organization that offers advanced certifications like CISSP and CCSP. Founded in 1989, it provides valuable resources, training, and events to help cybersecurity professionals excel and maintain ethical standards in their practices. Through its efforts, (ISC)² enhances global cybersecurity by equipping professionals with the knowledge and tools to succeed.
