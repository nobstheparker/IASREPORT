Philippine Computer Emergency Response Team (PHCERT)

Strengthening Cybersecurity for the Nation

Role

PHCERT handles cybersecurity incidents and supports organizations in mitigating threats.

Functions

  • Respond to cyberattacks and security breaches.
  • Provide training and resources for security best practices.
  • Collaborate with government and private institutions to combat cybercrime.

Impact

PHCERT enhances the country's preparedness against emerging cybersecurity threats by providing expertise and resources to address incidents effectively.
